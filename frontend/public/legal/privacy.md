5. Third-Party Services & Integrations
5.1 Supabase
We use Supabase to provide authentication, database, and file storage services for Rivue.ai. Your account information, uploaded files, and related metadata are securely stored and managed using Supabase’s infrastructure. Supabase implements industry-standard security practices, including encryption and role-based access controls.
5.2 Google Cloud Vision API
We utilize the Google Cloud Vision API to extract text from files you upload (such as PDFs or images). This processing enables Rivue.ai to generate study questions and other interactive content based on your uploaded material. Files are securely transmitted to Google Cloud Vision solely for this processing purpose. Google Cloud may retain de-identified log data for abuse-monitoring for up to 30 days; no Rivue.ai account identifiers are included in such logs.
5.3 OpenAI API
The OpenAI API is used to generate AI-powered questions and educational content from the text extracted from your uploads. Only the relevant extracted text (not your personal account details) is shared with OpenAI for this limited purpose, and only as needed to provide Platform features at your request. OpenAI retains input text for up to 30 days solely for abuse monitoring and does not use it to train its models.
5.4 Pinecon
We use Pinecone to store and index extracted text and related data as vector embeddings, enabling fast retrieval and efficient content generation for your courses. Pinecone is used exclusively for supporting the Platform’s core study features.
5.6 No Analytics, Ads, or Payment Processors
Rivue.ai does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or marketing SDKs, nor do we currently integrate any payment processors. Your data is not used for analytics, advertising, or payment processing purposes.
